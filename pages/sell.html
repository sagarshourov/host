Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to sell with You're Home?
Just 2.5% total fees compared to 6% with traditional realtors. On a $500K home, you save $17,500.
Do I need a real estate license?
No license required. You're selling your own property directly to buyers. Our licensed agents are available for support when needed.
What if I need help with paperwork?
We provide complete transaction support including contracts, escrow services, and closing coordination. Licensed agents available on-demand.
How do you verify buyers are qualified?
Every buyer must upload a pre-approval letter or get approved through our partner lenders before making offers. No unqualified offers.
What's included in the 2.5% fee?
Listing on our platform, professional photography, AI pricing analysis, transaction support, legal documentation, and escrow services.
Can I still use the MLS?
Yes! For an additional 1% fee, we can also list your property on MLS to reach traditional buyers while maintaining our direct-sale benefits.
